Privacy Policy

Countdown - Timer App for iOS
Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Countdown (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our countdown timer application.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Countdown names and descriptions you create
  • Target dates and times for your countdowns
  • App preferences and settings
  • Custom group names and colors

Automatically Collected Data

  • App usage analytics (crashes, performance)
  • Device information (iOS version, device type)
  • App version and session data

Data Not Collected

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Photos or camera access
  • Microphone or audio data

How We Use Your Information

Core App Functions

  • Store and sync your countdowns across your devices
  • Send local notifications for countdown events
  • Display countdowns in widgets and extensions

AI-Powered Features (Optional)

When you use AI suggestion features:

  • Countdown names and descriptions are sent to our secure servers
  • Data is forwarded to OpenAI (ChatGPT) to generate suggestions
  • Responses are sent back to enhance your experience
  • No personal identifying information is included

Data Processing Location

  • Core countdown data: Stored locally on your device
  • AI features: Processed via our AWS servers in the United States
  • Third-party AI: OpenAI servers (subject to OpenAI's privacy policy)

Data Sharing and Third Parties

Service Providers

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Hosts our API servers
  • OpenAI: Provides AI-powered suggestion features
  • No data is sold to third parties for marketing purposes

Data Retention

  • Local data: Remains on your device until you delete the app
  • Server data: AI queries are not permanently stored
  • Temporary processing data: Deleted within 30 days

Your Privacy Rights

Data Control

  • All countdown data can be deleted by deleting the app
  • AI features are optional and can be avoided
  • No account creation required

Data Access

  • You can view all your data within the app
  • No hidden data collection or storage

Data Security

Protection Measures

  • Local data stored using iOS secure storage
  • Server communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No sensitive personal data transmitted
  • API keys and credentials securely managed

Device Security

  • Data synced via Apple's App Groups (secure)
  • Relies on your device's built-in security features
  • No cloud backup of sensitive information

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app's functionality (countdown timers) is appropriate for all ages, and no personal data collection is required for core features.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any material changes through:

  • App Store update notes
  • In-app notifications for significant changes

Third-Party Privacy Policies

When using AI features, your data is also subject to:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • AWS Privacy Notice
